Who We Are

Rooted in Europe. Focused on the American Market.

KM Textiles was founded to bridge Europe’s textile-machinery expertise with the growing demand of North American manufacturers.
Based in and supported by a logistics hub within the EU, we partner with select European producers to supply high-grade weaving, spinning, dyeing, and finishing equipment to U.S. buyers — without the headaches of direct importation. Our hybrid presence — European headquarters + U.S. field representative — means you get the best of both worlds: European sourcing precision and American-style responsiveness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is KM Textiles based, and do you operate in the United States?

KM Textiles is headquartered in Europe, with strong distribution and logistics partnerships globally. While our base of operations is in Europe, we maintain  dedicated U.S. liaisons who travel regularly between regions to assist clients with installations, inspections, and after-sales support.

Do you manufacture the machinery you sell?

No, KM Textiles is a specialized distributor, not a manufacturer. We represent leading European textile machinery brands such as Picanol, Saurer, and Brückner, ensuring our clients receive only genuine, high-performance equipment directly from the source.

How does shipping and delivery from Europe to the U.S. work?

We partner with global logistics providers like DHL Global Forwarding, DB Schenker, and Kuehne + Nagel. Once your order is confirmed, we handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and inland transport. Most deliveries to the U.S. are completed within 6–10 weeks, depending on order size and shipping method.

Can you help with installation, setup, or maintenance?

Yes. Through our U.S.-based technical liaison and European service network, we provide full support — from installation guidance to operator training and spare parts sourcing. For larger installations, we can coordinate on-site support with manufacturer technicians.
